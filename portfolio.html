About Me

I move between two worlds that most people keep separate: the analytical world of data and the expressive world of design. I'm building a career that draws on all three strengths, and I'm looking for opportunities where careful, detail-oriented work is valued — whether that's behind a dashboard, a sketchbook, or a spreadsheet.
